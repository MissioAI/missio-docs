## Key Features

### Discord Integration

<Info>
  Missio.ai seamlessly integrates with Discord to streamline your support operations. This integration enables you to manage Discord server interactions efficiently and effectively.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Thread Management:** Automatically group messages into threads based on public and private discussions, as well as AI-predicted threads.
- **Metadata Management:** Easily manage thread metadata such as labels, priority, status, and assignment.
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** Monitor server activity in real-time to ensure prompt responses to inquiries and issues.

### Public and Private Threads

<Info>
  Manage both public and private threads within your Discord server. This ensures that you can handle all types of discussions appropriately and maintain organized support channels.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Public Threads:** Track and manage public conversations on your server, ensuring community-wide issues are addressed.
- **Private Threads:** Handle sensitive discussions privately, keeping important information secure and manageable.
- **AI-Predicted Threads:** Utilize AI to automatically group related messages into coherent threads, enhancing organization.

### Auto-Pilot for Discord

<Info>
  Auto-Pilot automates the creation and management of metadata for Discord threads, reducing the manual workload for your team and ensuring consistency.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Automatic Assignment:** Assign the correct team members to handle specific inquiries or tasks.
- **Setting Statuses and Priorities:** Automatically set the status and priority of threads based on predefined rules.
- **Drafting Responses:** Auto-draft responses using predefined templates and AI-generated suggestions.

### Advanced Search and Documentation Access

<Info>
  Easily search through your Discord messages and linked documentation to find relevant information quickly. This ensures that your team can provide accurate and timely support.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Search Messages:** Quickly search through Discord messages to find past discussions and resolutions.
- **Documentation Access:** Seamlessly access linked documentation and resources to provide accurate support information.

### Analytics and Insights

<Info>
  Gain valuable insights into your Discord support operations with comprehensive analytics and reporting tools. Use these insights to make data-driven decisions and improve your support processes.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**
- **Support Metrics:** Track key metrics such as response time, resolution time, and ticket volume.
- **Team Performance:** Analyze individual and team performance to identify areas for improvement.
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Measure customer satisfaction through feedback and surveys.
