## Key Features

### Email Integration

<Info>
  Missio.ai will seamlessly integrate with your email service to streamline your
  support operations. This integration will enable you to manage email
  interactions efficiently and effectively.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**

- **Thread Management:** Automatically group emails into threads, keeping your inbox organized and manageable.
- **Metadata Management:** Easily manage email thread metadata such as labels, priority, status, and assignment.
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** Monitor email activity in real-time to ensure prompt responses to inquiries and issues.

### Auto-Pilot for Email

<Info>
  Auto-Pilot will automate the creation and management of metadata for email
  threads, reducing the manual workload for your team and ensuring consistency.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**

- **Automatic Assignment:** Assign the correct team members to handle specific email inquiries.
- **Setting Statuses and Priorities:** Automatically set the status and priority of email threads based on predefined rules.
- **Drafting Responses:** Auto-draft responses using predefined templates and AI-generated suggestions.

### Advanced Search and Documentation Access

<Info>
  Easily search through your email messages and linked documentation to find
  relevant information quickly. This ensures that your team can provide accurate
  and timely support.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**

- **Search Emails:** Quickly search through email messages to find past discussions and resolutions.
- **Documentation Access:** Seamlessly access linked documentation and resources to provide accurate support information.

### Analytics and Insights

<Info>
  Gain valuable insights into your email support operations with comprehensive
  analytics and reporting tools. Use these insights to make data-driven
  decisions and improve your support processes.
</Info>

**Key Capabilities:**

- **Support Metrics:** Track key metrics such as response time, resolution time, and email volume.
- **Team Performance:** Analyze individual and team performance to identify areas for improvement.
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Measure customer satisfaction through feedback and surveys.
